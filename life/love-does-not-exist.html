Love is like magic,
It does not exist.

It bears all things,
But even boulders wither at the chafes of ropes.

Believes all things,
Not even new-borns are that gullible.

Hopes all things,
But it is despair that nulls the ache of a heart too weary to beat against the tides.

Endures all things,
But even mountains are eroded by air with time.

Love is like magic,
It does not exist.

Love is like magic,
It is found in the pursuit of its realness.

The work that goes into the illusion is the magic.

The work that goes into love is love.

To love is to pursue love.

Love is magic.
